Tema may be the one city that has it all. The larger than life city brings a lot to the Ghanaian cultural table, especially in terms of Rap. Sarkodie, Ponobiom, Yaw Siki, Omar Sterling, Mugeez, and Cris Waddle all hail from Tema, and there are scores of other hot young rappers we've uncovered that are either influential to rap as a whole or still very much at the top of their game.

Take a look at this list of rappers from Tema and vote for your favorite

1. B4BONAH

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Nsuo Bɛyɛ Buɔ Dɛn? (@b4bonah) on Nov 29, 2019 at 11:37pm PST

B4Bonah is a Ghanaian rapper. He stepped into the rap scene in 2017 by releasing his single 'Dear God'. Since then, he has released several hit songs, such as "Kpeme, "Higher", "Devil Is A Liar", "My Girl". Born in Tema, B4Bonah is undoubtedly one of the prominent young rappers in the country. His unbeatable and unique way of rapping has earned him thousands of fans not only in Ghana, but all across the globe. The talented rapper has a massive social media following, especially on Youtube where he is listened to and watched by millions.

Follow B4Bonah




2. KWESI ARTHUR

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by SON OF JACOB!!! (@kwesiarthur_) on Jan 12, 2020 at 5:36am PST

Kwesi Arthur is a rapper and songwriter who became a BET nominee in a short span of time. He is only 25 years old and best known for songs such as 'Anthem' which has over 1 million views on youtube. His debut single, 'Grind Day,' became a huge hit after he featured Sarkodie and Medikal in 2018. Kwesi Arthur was nominated for Best International Act at the 2018 BET Awards. With his growing fan following, Kwesi Arthur seems to be the next big thing in the rap world.

Follow Kwesi Arthur




3. AMON WEST

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Amon West (@amon.west) on Dec 8, 2019 at 1:59pm PST

Based in Tema, Amon West is on the rise, on the move, and heating up with his fante rap. Amon West has a rich familiar background in music that reaches back to the West(Ahanta West). His talent moved him to release an emotion-filled EP, 'Nothing To Lose', which features Tema music genius, B4Bonah. He is currently working with BoiiSam, Ponobiom, Kelvin Boy, Sarkodie, Gee Lou and Why Snitch.

Follow Amon West







4. 2FYNGERZGH

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by #GiDi_GiDi ft Fameye Out Soon! (@2fyngerzgh) on Nov 29, 2019 at 11:37pm PST

Tema rapper 2FYNGERZGH was first known for his viral songs -'Akorla borni', 'POSSESSED' - but those who came for the bars stayed for his keen production, his knack for melody and structure, keeping us - literally - on the hook. Last year, 2FYNGERZGH dropped 'Bounce', which held up to his high standard of lyric efficacy and relatability. 2FYNGERZGH is about to lead the year with a hard-hitting tune featuring Fameye, 'GIDI GIDI', which bodes well for 2FYNGERZGH visibility in 2020.

Follow 2FYNGERZGH







5. RAPHENZEE

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by RAPH ENZEE (@raphenzee) on Jul 11, 2019 at 12:00am PDT

A slick-tongued Tema ryhmer with an incredible eye for detail and a wicked sense of humor, RAPHENZEE made major moves over the last 12 months. He dropped 'Brodo schemes', his first EP which features Teephlow and Awuley Bembem. His work has been endorsed by Sarkodie and Jayso. He is undoubtedly one of the best rappers to watch out for in 2020.

Follow 2FYNGERZGH
